FAQs

How does the smart home system work?

Our system connects all your smart devices—lights, locks, thermostats, cameras—into one easy-to-use app. You can control everything remotely or set up automation rules to simplify your routine.

Can I control my home when I’m not there?

Yes! With internet access, you can manage and monitor your home from anywhere using your smartphone or tablet.

Is my data secure with your app?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and secure authentication to keep your personal data and device access safe.

What devices are compatible?

Our app supports a wide range of smart home devices from leading brands, including lights, thermostats, locks, cameras, and more. Check our compatibility page for full details.

Is there a monthly subscription?

The basic version of the app is free to use. We also offer premium features through an optional subscription for users who want advanced automation and analytics.

Can I use voice assistants?

Yes! Our system integrates smoothly with voice assistants like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri for hands-free control.

How difficult is the setup?

Setup is simple and user-friendly. Most users can connect and configure their devices in just a few minutes using our step-by-step guide in the app.

What happens if there’s a power outage?

Most smart devices will resume normal function when power is restored. For critical functions like security, we recommend devices with battery backup options.
